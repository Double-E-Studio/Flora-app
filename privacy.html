Privacy Policy

Last updated: 3 March 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Flora app (hereafter referred to as "Application") for iOS devices, created by Double E Studio (hereafter referred to as "Service Provider"). The Application is provided as a free service, "AS IS".

Information Collection

The Application does not collect any personal information. No registration or login is required to use the Application.

The only data stored is user preferences (such as bookmarked flowers), which are saved locally on the device and are never transmitted or shared externally.

Location Information

The Application does not request or collect precise real-time location information from your device at any time.

Third-Party Access

Since the Application does not collect personal information, no data is shared with any third parties. The Application operates entirely offline and does not communicate with external servers.

Internet Access

Flora does not require an internet connection to function. All flower content — images, descriptions, and fun facts — is bundled directly within the Application and stored on your device.

Children

The Application is not directed to children under the age of 13. The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us at doublee.studio.team@gmail.com so we can take appropriate action.

Security

The Application does not transmit or store personal information externally, which means there is no risk of unauthorised access to user data through the Application. All locally stored preferences are protected by the standard iOS device security model.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Your continued use of the Application after any modifications constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

Your Consent

By using the Application, you consent to the processing of information as described in this Privacy Policy — now and as amended in the future.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns regarding privacy while using the Application, please contact the Service Provider at doublee.studio.team@gmail.com.
